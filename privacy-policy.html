This Privacy Policy describes how MYANMAR 2D 3D (“we,” “us,” or “our”) collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile application (the “App”) on iOS or Android.

Advertising data

App preferences stored on your device

Technical and usage data

  • When you use features such as live results, history, calendar, dream book, your device communicates with our servers to fetch content.

We do not collect precise location data, contacts, photos from your camera roll, or microphone audio through the App.
